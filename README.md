# Instacart Grocery Basket Analysis
Analyzing customer purchasing behavior to inform targeted marketing stratgies using instacart transcation data 

## Business Problem
Instacart stakeholders want to better understand customer purchasing behvaior to desgin targeted marketing campaigns. this project explores patterns in customer activity, product preferences, and ordering habits to identify actionable customer segments. 

## Goal
* What are the busiest days of the week and hours of the day?
* At what times do customers spend the most money?
* With Instacart’s wide range of product prices, what recommendations can simplify price range groupings to better guide marketing efforts?
* Are there specific types of products that are more popular than others?
Do ordering habits vary based on a customer’s loyalty status?
* Are there differences in ordering habits based on a customer’s region and profile?

## Key Insight
* Customers order most frequenctly on weekends and evenings
* High frequency uers show string brand loyalty in specific categories
* Budget conscious users cluster around mid-range pricing tiers
* Families tend to purchase more bulk and staple items
